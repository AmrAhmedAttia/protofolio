Hi, I'm Amr Ahmed

UI/UX Designer & IoT Developer

I am a university student at Menoufia National University, specializing in UI/UX design, IoT, and software development. I focus on building real products that solve problems, not just designing interfaces or writing code.

In UI/UX, I led the NTI graduation project Clothing Storage and collaborated with a team to develop a language learning application. I also created personal projects including a Marvel poster, a professional CV design, and a full application capable of detecting anemia. These experiences taught me how to combine user-centered design with practical, functional solutions.

In IoT, I completed projects at ITI using the Master of Things platform. I developed a mobile light intensity sensor, a Baby Care monitoring system, and a Smart Parking solution. Each project involved hardware-software integration, programming microcontrollers, and creating systems that provide real value.

I am skilled in UI/UX tools like Figma and Adobe XD, and in IoT platforms, microcontrollers, and sensors. I enjoy turning ideas into practical applications, working collaboratively in teams, and tackling real-world challenges.

I am open to internships, collaborative projects, and opportunities where I can apply my skills to deliver functional, user-focused solutions.
